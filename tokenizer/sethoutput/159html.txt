text of hr 695 the quotsafequot bill

a bill
to amend title 18 united states code to affirm the rights of americans
to use and sell encryption and to relax export controls on encryption


be it enacted by the senate and house of representatives of the united
states of america in congress assembled

section 1 short title

this act may be cited as the quotsecurity and freedom through encryption
safe actquot

quot1 the terms person state wire communications electronic

quot1672802 freedom to use encryption

quotsubject to section 2805 it shall be lawful for any person within

quot1672803 freedom to sell encryption

quotsubject to section 2805 it shall be lawful for any person within
