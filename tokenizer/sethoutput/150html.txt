cdt policy posts

cdt policy posts are the regular news publication of the center for democracy and
technology they are designed to deliver accurate uptodate information on public policy
issues affecting civil liberties online  cdts policy posts a leading resource for policy makers press industry representatives students and others who are interested in legal and policy issues affecting the internet and other interactive media
